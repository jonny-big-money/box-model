How to cook food

Boiling How to do it: Fill a pot with water. Bring it to a rolling boil. Add food (e.g., pasta, vegetables). Cook until tender, then drain. Frying How to do it: Heat oil in a pan. Add food (e.g., chicken, vegetables). Cook until golden and crispy, turning occasionally.

how to cook food (pt 2)

Baking How to do it: Preheat the oven to the desired temperature. Place food (e.g., bread, cakes) in a baking dish. Bake until cooked through and golden brown. Steaming How to do it: Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil. Place food in a steamer basket above the water. Cover and steam until tender.
